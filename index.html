Understanding Scale Through Data

Revolutionary Era art ranges dramatically in size—from intricate miniatures to grand canvases. This interactive tool lets you visualize these differences at actual size, comparing each artwork to familiar reference objects.

Use the measurement converter to explore dimensions in your preferred units. Click any artwork in the gallery below to view it at three different scales: actual size, fit-to-screen, and with size references.

Mark artworks with the star icon to add them to Favorites, then compare two or three side by side from the FAVORITES tab.
