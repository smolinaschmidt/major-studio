Exploring the Scale of Art History

This project analyzes data from The Revolutionary Era Art Collections, focusing on how the physical size of the artworks has changed or remained consistent across decades, from 18th-century miniatures to larger canvases. We use real dimensions to visually illustrate these scale differences.

The dataset includes a mix of paintings and miniatures by different artists. Scroll down to view the grid below and explore the proportions to scale.
